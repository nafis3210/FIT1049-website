About

This Webpage showcases various Action Figures that were collected by the owner of this web page through out his time in Australia. The Owner has collected these figures from various sources. Some of these action figures may be attached to Key Chains whereas the others are purely collectibles.

Action-Figure #1

Princess Leia hails from the wonderous world of Star Wars. Princess Leia Organa was one of the Rebel Alliance's greatest leaders, fearless on the battlefield and dedicated to ending the tyranny of the Empire.

Action-Figure #2

Captain Marvel is a superhero from...Yeah, The Marvel Universe. Her real name is Carol Danvers. She is also an integral part of the superhero group The Avengers.

Action-Figure #3

Smiley the Keychain is your ever-reliable keychain that you can buy at any supermarket around you. The Special thing about Smiley is that the figure is gives off some serious positive vibes to its bearer.

Action-Figure #4

Just Like Captain Marvel, Jean Grey also belongs to the Marvel Universe. But Instead of being part of the Avengers Jean is part of another superhero group called X-Men, which is led by professor X. She is also considered one of the most powerful characters in the Marvel Universe.

Action-Figure #5

Yoda is a legendary Jedi Master with a stronger connection with the Force than most. Small in size but wise and powerful, he trained Jedi for over 800 years.

Action-Figure #6

Hulk is a giant green monster belonging to the Marvel Universe. Hulk is most infamous for his rage. The angrier Hulk gets the stronger Hulk gets. It is considered that Hulks strenght knows no limits.

Action-Figure #7

This guy is not your average Ninja. He hacks and he slashes, there are very few opponents that can face him and live. He was trained atop mt Fuji, Japan, where he honed all his Ninja skills to the maximum ability.

Action-Figure #8

Ichigo Kurosaki is the protagonist of the Manga series Bleach. He receieves Soul reaping power after befriending Rukia Kuchiki, the Soul Reaper assigned to patrol around the city of Karakura Town.
